About Me

Hello! Thanks for taking the time to look through my portfolio. I am a budding gameplay programmer currently earning my master's at SMU Guildhall in Plano, Texas. I'm thrilled to have the opportunity to explore my areas of interests in games, especially procedural content generation, and artificial intelligence, and I'm even more excited to bring what I've learned at the Guildhall into the games industry next spring.

So much of my interest in video games is sparked by the child-like wonder I felt growing up just exploring games with my brothers. Whether we were completing a particularly hard level without a strategy guide, or finding an obscure easter egg, or making up fun new ways to exploit mechanics in the game, that sense of discovery is what drove me to play games. I hope to continue to deliver that sense of wonder to players around the world.

Outside of work, I like to play RPGs, puzzle games, strategy games, but beyond any genre I enjoy games that find ways to feel new and exciting. Some of my favorite games right now are Subnautica, Breath of the Wild, Stardew Valley, and Ori and the Blind Forest. Outside of games, I like to spend my free time baking, drawing, and camping.
